Hi, I’m Aakankshya Shrestha, a Master’s student in Computer Science at uco. I’m passionate about Human-Computer Interaction and immersive technologies, especially designing engaging experiences with AR/VR and game-based learning.

My work revolves around creating interactive systems that are not only functional but also fun and intuitive, combining user-centered design with hands-on prototyping in Unity and other platforms. I’m excited to continue exploring how technology can enhance learning and human experiences, and I’m looking forward to pursuing PhD research in HCI and interactive systems.
